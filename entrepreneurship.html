Entrepreneurship

The Biomedical Technology and Cell Therapy Research Laboratory is a proud promotor of industrial contributions. Many technologies become spin off companies, some of these spin offs are listed below.

MangoGen Pharma

MangoGen develops gene-delivering medical devices to promote wound healing. The novel technology involves a gene-delivering coronary stent including a coating of encapsulated genetically modified baculoviruses to accelerate the healing processes and prevent in-stent restenosis.

Homepage LinkedIn
Micropharma

Micropharma is a biopharmaceutical company specializing in cholesterol lowering technologies. The leading product, cardioviva is sold across the globe.

Homepage LinkedIn
Nanora Pharma

Nanorapharma USA is a pharmaceutical company that is involved in developing a polymeric nano-delivery vehicle for therapeutics like small interfering RNA (siRNA), pDNA (plasmid DNA), shRNA (short hair-pin RNA), antisense oligonucleotides (ASOs), chemotherapeutic drugs and small molecules. The delivery vehicle developed is a platform technology that can cater its suitability towards different applications, such as targeting cancer, neurodegenerative diseases or for oral delivery of nutraceuticals.

Homepage
Proviva Pharma

Proviva Pharma is invested in the long term health of citizens globally. They work to understand the role of the microbiome in overall health and develop innovative probiotics for a plethora of disorders including heart health, gastrointestinal diseases, bone, obesity and metabolic disease.
